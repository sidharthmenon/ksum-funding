Funding

The role of KSUM in fostering the startup investor ecosystem in Kerala is critical for startups to sustain. As the apex government organisation promoting entrepreneurship development, KSUM facilitates access to equity funds for startups through Venture Capitalists and Angel Investors. Schemes such as Fund of fund has made available a corpus fund of around 1000 crore ready for the tech startups to scale up. Investor Exposure programs hosted by KSUM gives a platform for connecting potential investors or HNIs with the experienced startup investors.

Investor Cafe

Investor Cafe is a one on one meeting with investors for startup founders to raise equity funds. This is scheduled on the last Wednesdays of every month at the Integrated Startup Complex, Kochi.
